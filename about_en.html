ABOUT US

THE HABICHUELA

We are a store specializing in high-end food: delicatessen and gourmet. Where you will find very varied products such as: cold meats, sausages, cuts of meat, artisanal, national and imported cheeses, coffee, oils and more. All with high quality and exclusivity.

Our goal is to fully satisfy and meet the expectations of our customers and that is why we work every day to always offer the best. What is truly important is in the content of all our gourmet and delicatessen products with the aim of surprising you with their flavour, aroma, texture and colour, as was the case in the past and which is so difficult to find today. For us, natural and artisanal production are the keys to quality and exquisiteness.


100% natural food


We offer you a selection of more than 100 Gourmet Products for all budgets, with an excellent quality/price ratio. In addition, we have a large stock of all our products, guaranteeing a fast and quality delivery service to our customers. We are specialized in Gourmet products throughout the year for a special occasion such as Christmas dinner, company party, weddings, baptisms or communions.
